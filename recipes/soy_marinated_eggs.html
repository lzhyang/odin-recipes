Soy-Marinated Eggs

Description

Jammy eggs in a sweet and savory marinade.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/8 cup honey
  • 1/8 cup water
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 cup scallions,sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1 dash sesame oil
  • 1 dash black vinegar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch white pepper

Steps

  1. Softboil the eggs and transfer them to an ice bath.
  2. While the eggs are cooling, prep and combine the rest of the ingredients in a bowl. Mix until the honey is dissolved.
  3. Peel the eggs and gently drop them into the marinade.
  4. Marinate for up to 4 days, turning the eggs every so often to make sure they marinate evenly.
  5. Serve on top of rice with the marinade spooned over or as a side dish.
